Big gift for J&K: Amit Shah flags off Delhi-Katra Vande Bharat Express, travel time cut by 4 hours

Vande Bharat Express, known as Train 18, has been inaugurated by Home Minister Amit Shah.

Home Minister Amit Shah launched the Train 18 on Thursday from Delhi.

The first commercial run of the Delhi-Katra Vande Bharat Express will be on October 5 and booking of tickets is now open on the IRCTC website.

The train will bring down the travel time between Delhi and Katra, the last station on the way to Vaishno Devi temple, to eight hours from 12 hours.

Article 370 was a mistake, says senior lawyer Harish Salve

Senior Indian lawyer Harish Salve on Wednesday endorsed Modi government's decision to revoke Article 370 to withdraw the special status of Jammu and Kashmir as a means of correcting a "mistake".

The Kashmir Constitution says Kashmir is an integral part of India, not just the Indian Constitution. It is Pakistan-occupied Kashmir that is the disputed territory, Harish Salve said.

Harish Salve was addressing the media at the Indian High Commission in London.

Speaking to India Today in London on the abrogation of Harish Salve said, I have been long-time votary of getting rid of 370. It was a mistake to allow it. Bigger mistake to allow it to fester. Calling the suggestion of discussion and debate on the matter, non-sense he said, doing it in one shot was the right decision.
